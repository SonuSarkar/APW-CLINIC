Orthopedic Condition

These conditions are in form of injuries and diseases that affect the musculoskeletal system. This includes the muscles, bones, nerves, joints, ligaments, tendons, and some other connective tissues. Damage to any of these tissues can come from chronic orthopedic diseases or from an injury.

Stress Reduction

Stress is a state of emotional, mental, or physical tension. It may come from any moment or thought that puts you in the condition of feeling frustrated, angry, or nervous. It puts a bad effect on your mental health.

De-TOxification

As the toxins damage your enzymes, they prevent the production of hemoglobin in your blood, which may result in fast aging. It can also lead to the failure of energy production. Such basic failures in your body system may take your body to serious illnesses and diseases like cancer.

Gastroentrology

These are the most common problems affecting the organs like the colon and rectum. Constipation, irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), nausea, food poisoning, gas, bloating, and diarrhea are common symptoms of such failures.

Obesity

Obesity is a state of the body involving an excessive amount of body fat. It isn't just a cosmetic concern. It's a medical problem that increases the risks and chances of other diseases and health problems, such as cardiac issues, diabetes, high blood pressure, and certain cancers.

Dermatology & Hair Condition

Diseases like dermatology include common skin spots to severe skin infections, which are the outcome of a range of things, such as infections, allergens, medications, system disorders. Hair disorders also range from dandruff to hair loss.

Nurological Disorder

Neurological disorders are disorders that affect the brain as well as the entire nerves throughout the human body. Paralysis, muscle weakness, and Seizures are some of its symptoms.

Giatric Care

Geriatric illnesses are multifactorial conditions that are prevalent in older adults. Geriatric illnesses develop when an individual experiences accumulated impairments in multiple body systems that compromise their compensatory ability.

Immunity

Immunity is a complex biological system endowed with the capacity to recognize and tolerate whatever belongs to the body and to recognize and reject what don't. Disorders in immunity prevent the body to fight common diseases and micro-organisms which can be prevented with the immune body.

Opthalmology

Ophthalmology deals with the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of eye and sight diseases. The consequences of its disorders can lead to slight sight loss or complete slight loss

Psychosomatic Disorder

Psychosomatic disorders are psychological conditions including the occurrence of physical symptoms, usually lacking a medical explanation. People with these conditions face some physical challenges which affect their ability to function well.
